# Smart Pharmacy Management System

## Abstract

Smart Pharmacy Management System is a web-based application that streamlines pharmacy operations by managing medicines, inventory, billing, sales, suppliers, and customer records. The system provides real-time stock monitoring, automated billing, and efficient record management, reducing manual work and improving operational accuracy.

---

## Keywords

**Pharmacy Management, Inventory Management, Billing System, Stock Management, Healthcare, Python, SQLite, Streamlit**

---

## 1. Introduction

Smart Pharmacy Management System is designed to digitize pharmacy operations by managing medicines, sales, inventory, suppliers, and customer records through a single platform.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Manual pharmacy management is time-consuming, prone to errors, and makes inventory tracking and billing inefficient.

---

## 3. Objectives

* Manage medicine inventory.
* Automate billing and sales.
* Track stock in real time.
* Maintain supplier and customer records.
* Reduce manual workload.

---

## 4. Methodology

1. Add medicines to inventory.
2. Monitor stock levels.
3. Process customer sales.
4. Generate bills automatically.
5. Update inventory and records.

---

## 5. Literature Review

Traditional pharmacy systems mainly focus on billing or inventory separately. Smart Pharmacy Management System integrates inventory, billing, sales, suppliers, and customer management into a single platform.
